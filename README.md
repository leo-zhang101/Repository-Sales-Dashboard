# Retail Sales Dashboard (Tableau)

## Overview
This project demonstrates an end-to-end data analytics workflow using a synthetic retail dataset.  

The objective is to simulate a real-world business scenario and generate actionable insights through data analysis and visualisation.

---

## Key Metrics
- Total Revenue: $101M+
- Total Orders: 97,650
- Average Order Value (AOV): $1,038

---

## Dashboard
The Tableau dashboard provides a clear and concise view of business performance.

It includes:
- Monthly revenue trend analysis
- Top 10 products by revenue
- Summary KPI indicators for quick decision-making

---

## Key Insights
- Revenue shows clear seasonality, with a strong peak in Q4 (November–December)
- February consistently records the lowest performance
- The top 10 products contribute approximately 37% of total revenue, indicating moderate concentration risk
- Profitability varies significantly across product categories, suggesting pricing and discount strategies could be optimised
- Regional performance is uneven, with certain regions underperforming and presenting growth opportunities
